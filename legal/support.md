## Common questions

**How do I scan a barcode?**
Open the app → Scan tab → point your camera at the barcode. Make sure you've granted camera permission (Settings → Scan to Cart → Camera).

**Why are some prices estimates?**
Right now we have real prices for Kroger (when you connect your account). Other stores show estimates while we partner with each chain. Connect Kroger in Profile for live prices on those items.

**How do I connect my Kroger account?**
Profile tab → Kroger section → Connect Kroger. Sign in with your Kroger.com credentials. After connecting you can send your in-app cart directly to Kroger.com.

**How do I delete my account?**
Profile tab → Account section → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data.

**Does the app work without an internet connection?**
Mostly yes — scanning, browsing your cart, and viewing history work offline. Search results, price comparisons, and Kroger features need internet.

**My nutrition data isn't syncing to Apple Health.**
Profile → Health integrations → toggle Apple Health. Make sure to allow the permission prompts. We only write data — we don't read your existing Health entries.
