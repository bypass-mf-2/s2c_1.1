# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-05-06
**App:** Scan to Cart
**Developer:** Trevor Goodwill

This Privacy Policy explains what information Scan to Cart collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

## Information we collect

**Account information** — When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your email address and (if you choose to share it) your name. We use this to create your account and personalize your experience.

**Scanned products and shopping activity** — We store the barcodes you scan, items you add to your cart or log, store preferences, nutrition goals, and budget settings. This data is associated with your account and used to provide tracking and personalized recommendations.

**Device data** — We collect basic device information (iOS version, device model, app version) to diagnose crashes and improve performance.

**Location** — When you use the "Find nearby stores" feature, we use your device's location to find grocery stores near you. Your location is used in real time and not stored.

**Camera** — We use your camera to scan product barcodes. Camera images and frames are processed on your device and never sent to our servers.

**Apple Health** — If you connect Apple Health, we write nutrition data (calories and macros) for items you log to your Health app. We do not read your existing Health data.

**Kroger account** — If you connect your Kroger account, we store an authentication token (not your password) to add items to your Kroger online cart on your behalf. You can disconnect at any time.

## How we use your information

- Provide core app features (scanning, tracking, recommendations)
- Personalize your experience (greetings, suggested products)
- Compare prices and find nearby stores
- Diagnose crashes and improve the app
- Send items to Kroger when you choose to

We do **not** sell your personal information. We do **not** share it with advertisers. We do **not** use it for marketing emails.

## Service providers we use

- **Supabase** — stores your account data and shopping history (encrypted at rest)
- **RevenueCat** — manages subscriptions
- **Open Food Facts, USDA FoodData Central** — public food databases (we send only the barcode or search term, never personal data)
- **Apple Maps / Apple Health** — Apple's on-device services
- **Kroger** — only when you connect your account; we send only what's required to manage your cart
- **Sentry** — anonymized crash reports (no personal data attached)

## Your rights

- **Access and edit your data** — Use the "Edit profile" option in Settings
- **Delete your account and all data** — Use "Delete account" in Settings (Profile → Account → Delete account). This permanently removes everything within 30 days.
- **Disconnect services** — Disconnect Kroger or Apple Health at any time from Settings

## Children

Scan to Cart is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

## Changes

We may update this policy. The "Effective Date" above will reflect the most recent revision. Material changes will be notified in-app.
